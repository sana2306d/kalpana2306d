Here is how to make an origami cube with six sheets of square paper. It doesn't matter what color paper you have, or the size of the paper. Just make sure your paper isn't too thick. Each fold will be in a seperate step with text that tells what to do. I apologize for the blurry pictures. They were taken on my IPod Touch
