How to fold an origami bell flower?

Know anything about Bell Flowers? Bell Flowers grow in the shade and come in many different colors like blue, pink, purple or white. They look pretty both in pictures and real life! Wanna create something lovely today? Follow these steps to make your very own origami bell flower!

STEP 1:

Start with a square piece of paper.

STEP 2:

Fold from top left corner to the bottom right corner then unfold.

STEP 3:

Fold from top right corner to the bottom left corner then unfold.

STEP 4:

Turn the paper over. Fold from the left edge to the right edge then unfold.

STEP 5:

Fold the top edge down to the bottom edge then unfold.

STEP 6:

Rotate the paper, meet the corners then flatten the paper.

STEP 7:

Fold the bottom corner upward then unfold.

STEP 8:

Flip the left side of the paper, open the whole then flatten it.

STEP 9:

Fold the left and right bottom corners to the center then unfold.

STEP 10:

Open the whole and push the crease in.

STEP 11:

Fold upward and diagonal the left and right bottom corners.

STEP 12:

Fold in the flaps.

STEP 13:

Turn the paper over, flip another left side of the paper and repeat the same steps on this side from steps 9-12.

Last Step:

Open the paper, and bend in the side corners to emphasize the petals and your origami bell flower is ready.
