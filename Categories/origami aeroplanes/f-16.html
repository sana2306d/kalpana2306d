How to Fold an Origami F-16 Plane?

In this instructable, I will teach you how to fold the plain awesome origami F-16! This model is not nearly as hard to fold as it looks, so don't be deterred by its complex appearance. Despite the sad fact that this particular plane doesn't fly too well, it never fails to impress. If you want to see my youtube video on how to make this same model, just click How To Fold an Origami F-16 to see a tutorial that is slightly easier to follow. So lets get started!

STEP 1:

Take a Piece of Printer Paper and Fold It in Half Lengthwise, Then Unfold

STEP 2:

Fold in the Diagonals

STEP 3:

Fold the Top of the Paper Down at the Point Where All Creases Intersect

STEP 4:

Fold the Top Edge Down About a Quarter Inch or So

STEP 5:

Fold the Bottom Edge Up, Between a Quarter Inch and a Half Inch

STEP 6:

Squash Fold Along the Creases

STEP 7:

Form the Right Tail

STEP 8:

Form the Right Wing

STEP 9:

Repeat Steps 7 and 8 on the Left Side

STEP 10 :

Shape the Left Wing and Form the Left Tail

STEP 11:

Shape the Left Tail.

STEP 12 :

Shape the Right Wing and Form the Right Tail

STEP 13 :

Shape the Fuselage

STEP 14 :

Form the Tail Fin

STEP 15 :

Shape the Plane.

STEP 16 :

Finishing the Shape

STEP 17 :

Fold Up the Winglets

Last Step:

Have Fun!!! (+flying Tips:)
