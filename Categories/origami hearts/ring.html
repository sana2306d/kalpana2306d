How to make an origami heart ring?

Give a gift of wearable book art by folding up an origami heart ring. For Valentine’s, Galantine’s or Palantine’s…who needs a reason for paper love?! Fiddly but ever so impressive. You'll need: Origami paper 10cm x 10cm (4″ x 4″) that's it!

STEP 1:

Fold in half squareways, and open out.

STEP 2:

Fold bottom edge up to middle crease.

STEP 3:

And what's now the bottom crease up to the middle crease too.

STEP 4:

Turn the paper 180 degrees.

STEP 5:

And repeat steps 2 and 3.

STEP 6:

Open out and fold in half squareways the opposite way to the very first fold we made at the beginning.

STEP 7:

Unfold and turn 90 degrees.

STEP 8:

Fold down the top crease line, away from you.

STEP 9:

Draw down the top left corner to align with the centre crease.

STEP 10 :

Draw the top right corner down to meet at the centre too

STEP 11:

Flip over, keeping point at the top.

STEP 12 :

Fold the point down as shown above.

STEP 13 :

Flip over. Draw your thumb up the centre crease on the left hand side, to open up the fold and then press it flat.

STEP 14 :

The crease should line up with the horizontal fold at the top.

STEP 15 :

Repeat on the right hand side.

STEP 16 :

Fold the top left wing down at an angle of 45 degrees to create a little triangle.

STEP 17 :

Repeat on the right hand side.

STEP 18 :

Tuck the remaining corners underneath neatly.

STEP 19 :

You can see that a heart shape has formed on the other side.

STEP 20 :

Flip back to where we were, and fold the first crease back on itself.

STEP 21 :

Then pinch that double thickness and fold it up to the next line.

STEP 22 :

And make another fold upwards.

STEP 23 :

And another.

STEP 24 :

We're ready to form the long strip into a ring shape. Gently roll the paper, bending it into a circle use a round pencil if that helps.

STEP 25 :

When the paper is nicely pliable, narrow down the top right edge by folding it in as shown.

STEP 26 :

At the left hand end, ease open the pocket and slide the right end in, completing the Origami Heart Ring.

STEP 27 :

Tweak to fit.

Last Step:

Happy folding dear paper lovers.
