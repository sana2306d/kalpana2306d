How to fold a winged and origami heart?

This origami heart with wings designed by Francis Ow is an easy to intermediate model. This origami winged heart is great for decorating cards for Valentineâ€™s Day, birthdays, anniversaries or other celebrations. Paper: the best type of paper for this origami heart is regular origami paper. Preferably the paper should have red on one side and white on the other. Most variety packs of origami paper will have this.

STEP 1:

This is the front of our origami paper, we want the heart to be this color.

STEP 2:

This is the back of our origami paper, which is usually white.

STEP 3:

Fold the bottom edge up to the top edge.

STEP 4:

Unfold the previous step.

STEP 5:

Fold the right edge over to the left edge.

STEP 6:

Unfold the previous step.

STEP 7:

Fold the bottom edge up to the central horizontal crease.

STEP 8:

Fold the top edge down to the central horizontal crease.

STEP 9:

Unfold the top flap.

STEP 10 :

Flip the paper over to the other side, from left to right.

STEP 11:

Diagonally fold the bottom right point aligning with the central vertical crease.

STEP 12 :

Diagonally fold the bottom left point, aligning with the central vertical crease.

STEP 13 :

Fold the right edge over to meet the central vertical crease.

STEP 14 :

Repeat on the left.

STEP 15 :

Unfold the previous two folds.

STEP 16 :

Unfold the bottom right section.

STEP 17 :

Refold the top right section, but reverse fold the diagonal crease that already exists.

STEP 18 :

The completed reverse fold.

STEP 19 :

Flatten the rest of the right section.

STEP 20 :

Repeat steps 16 to 19 on the left side.

STEP 21 :

Flip the model over to the other side.

STEP 22 :

Fold the top section down whilst part of the other side remains in place.

STEP 23 :

Flatten the paper, creating two new diagonal creases at the top.

STEP 24 :

Fold the lower section up along the pre-existing horizontal crease, whilst opening the section above. You will be forming two new diagonal creases.

STEP 25 :

Neatly flatten the paper, squashing the diagonal folds inside.

STEP 26 :

Start forming the heart shape. Fold the two top sections into points.

STEP 27 :

Finish forming the heart shape by folding the top two points down a little.

STEP 28 :

Fold the bottom edge up to the top (of that section). The bottom of the heart remains underneath.

STEP 29 :

Unfold the previous step.

STEP 30 :

Fold the bottom edge up to meet the crease you created in the previous step.

STEP 31 :

Unfold the previous step, then fold the bottom edge up so that the creases align as shown.

STEP 32 :

Unfold the previous step.

STEP 33 :

Fold the flap up.

STEP 34 :

Now concertina this flap by folding the top edge down to the top-most crease. Continue this until you have a concertina or fan shape.

Last Step:

The winged origami heart is complete!
