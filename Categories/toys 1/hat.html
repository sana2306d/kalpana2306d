How to make a origami hat?

Follow these instructions to create your unique origami hat. You will be learning how to craft some lovely paper hats using our creative ideas, inspiration, and artistic hands. The only material needed for this activity is a square piece of paper. It is also a kid-friendly tutorial; young children will surely accomplish this. Create other paper hats in different colors to decorate your study table or your favorite spot in your room. Think of more decorations, including attractive colors, glitters, or gemstones, that are perfect for taking your origami paper hat to the next level. Step 1 Step 1: Get a regular

STEP 1:

Get a regular piece of rectangular paper.

STEP 2:

Fold the paper in half lengthwise, then unfold.

STEP 2:

Fold the paper in half again from top to the bottom.

STEP 4:

Fold the left and right corners to the center to form a triangle.

STEP 5:

Fold up the bottom flap along the base of the triangle.

STEP 6:

Turn the paper over.

STEP 7:

Like Step 5, fold up the bottom flap.

Last Step:

Put the hat on and you're done! Click "Like" below if you like this origami hat!
