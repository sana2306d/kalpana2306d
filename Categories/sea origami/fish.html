How to make an origami fish?

The simplicity of this origami fish model is what makes it so nice (in our opinion!). You can leave your paper fish plain or decorate it with colouring pencils, or draw a little face on him.

STEP 1:

This is the front of our origami paper, our origami fish will end up being this colour.

STEP 2:

This is the back of the origami paper, which is sometimes white. Start this side up

STEP 3:

Fold the right edge over to the left edge.

STEP 4:

Unfold the previous step.

STEP 5:

Fold the top left and right corners diagonally in to the centre.

STEP 6:

Fold the left and right lower edges in to the centre.

STEP 7:

Fold the bottom edge up to the top point.

STEP 8:

Fold the bottom left and right corners diagonally to the centre.

STEP 9:

Unfold the last 2 steps.

STEP 10 :

Reverse fold the creases you made in step 8.

STEP 11:

Bring the bottom edge up, squashing the lower left and right flaps inside.

STEP 12 :

Fold the top flap down in half.

STEP 13 :

Fold the top left and right corners of the front flap diagonally down.

STEP 14 :

Optionally, to secure the tail, reverse the flaps behind.

STEP 15 :

Flip the paper over to the other side, the origami fish is complete!

Last Step:

You can draw a face, fins or scales, or maybe even colourful washi tape could be used to decorate your new origami fish friend.
