HOW to make an origami octopus?

The last flat octopus was made from orange origami, so this time I will go straight to the attack in red ( laughs ) By the way, here is how to fold a flat octopus origami.

STEP 1:

Fold it in a rectangle so that the color is on the outside and spread it out.

STEP 2:

Turn the paper 90 degrees, fold it into a rectangle in the same way, and unfold it.

STEP 3:

Turn it over and fold it into a triangle so that the color is on the inside.

STEP 4:

Turn the paper 90 degrees, fold it into a right angle and unfold it in the same way.

STEP 5:

Fold it as shown in the picture.

STEP 6:

Fold it along the center line as shown in the photo. (The one who is fluttering is below )

STEP 7:

Turn it over and fold it in the same way as before.

STEP 8:

Open once and open the bag as shown in the picture ( 4 places in total )

STEP 9:

Place it as shown in the picture.

STEP 10 :

Fold it as shown in the picture and open it once.

STEP 11:

Use the creases to open as shown in the photo.

STEP 12 :

Turn it over, make a crease in the same way, and unfold it.

STEP 13 :

Turn over the paper and fold it in two places on the front and back as before.

STEP 14 :

Turn the paper over to make it look like a photo, and fold the bottom side even finer. ( 4 places in total )

STEP 15 :

Fold up the lower paper. ( 4 places in total )

STEP 16 :

Make a notch with scissors while returning the four folded parts.

STEP 17 :

] Eight legs are completed.

Last Step:

The "three-dimensional octopus" is completed by adjusting the shape while pulling the head part with your fingers.
