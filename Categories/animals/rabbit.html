How to make an Origami rabbit?

Pick up a piece of paper. Do you see anything? Like most people, you think of a blank page or canvas as a place to write. A skilled origami specialist, though, might see a lot of possibilities: a rabbit, a crane, a dog, and even a turtle. Your mastery of folding only constrains a range of options. Origami is the best technique to create beauty seemingly out of nothing. It is derived from two Japanese terms that indicate folding and paper.

STEP 1:

Start with a square piece of origami paper with white side up.

STEP 2:

Fold the paper in half by folding the bottom corner to the top corner, then unfold.

STEP 3:

Now fold the left corner to the right corner, then unfold.

STEP 4:

Fold a small portion of the triangle base up as shown.

STEP 5:

Fold the left and right bottom edges up at the center as shown. These are the origami rabbit ears.

STEP 6:

Turn the figure over

STEP 7:

Fold the top corner of the head down as shown.

Last Step:

Fold the bottom corner of the chin up as shown, then draw on the face and you're done. Now you know how to make an origami rabbit!
